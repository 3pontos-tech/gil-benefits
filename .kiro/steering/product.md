# Product Overview

This is a Laravel-based appointment booking and consultation management platform built with a modular architecture. The application serves multiple user types through dedicated Filament admin panels:

- **Admin Panel**: System administration and oversight
- **Company Panel**: Business/organization management
- **Consultant Panel**: Individual consultant management
- **User Panel**: End-user appointment booking and management
- **Guest Panel**: Public-facing landing and registration

## Core Features

- **Appointment Management**: Booking, scheduling, and state machine-driven appointment workflows
- **Multi-tenant Architecture**: Company-based tenancy with user roles and permissions
- **Billing Integration**: Stripe-powered subscription and payment processing
- **Consultant Management**: Individual consultant profiles and availability
- **CRM Integration**: HighLevel CRM integration for lead and opportunity management
- **Multi-language Support**: English and Portuguese (pt_BR) localization

## Business Model

The platform operates on a subscription-based model with different billing plans for companies and individual users, managed through Stripe integration with automated webhook handling.
